About MIT WPU

MIT World Peace University (MIT WPU) is a premier educational institution in India, committed to providing a world-class education that empowers students to make a positive impact in the world.

Our Vision

To be a leading university that offers value-based quality education to create an enlightened and progressive society.

Our Mission

To create a synergy of education and research by offering innovative programs and fostering a spirit of inquiry and creativity among students.

Our Programs

We offer a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various fields such as engineering, management, law, pharmacy, and more.
